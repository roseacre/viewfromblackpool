Other Sustainability Ideas

This year we're officially joining Ikea's Live Lagom Community (opens in a new tab). We'll be sharing what we've learned here.

Live Lagom won't be the first time we've tried to introduce more environmentally friendly, sustainable practices into our lives. Some of our efforts for the WildLife Trust's 30 Day's Wild were recorded on our Hamster Days blog (opens in a new tab). You'll need to look at the updates for June 2017.

And we do pick up other ideas along the way that we try out.

We're contemplating a bit of natural dying and rag rug making after being inspired by a workshop at a recent Wildlife Trust Supporters' Day and thanks to the unexpected acquisition of several old tablecloths. The same Supporters' Day also demonstrated wildlife pond making so, who knows, you might be able to follow us trying that. It'll all be here whatever we attempt.
