Our Town

This will be the space where we share pictures and some historical snippets about the Fylde Coast.

While setting up the now defunct Blackpool Methodist Circuit website, I'd included a section on the history of the churches and always thought that I would restructure it and add some more content. That didn't happen. However it seems a shame not to make the history more widely available. I know I benefit from gems that others publish when doing family history research. Hopefully others will find this section of interest, when I get around to setting it up.

We've got a number of photographers in the family so undoubtedly we'll take an opportunity to share photographs of Blackpool and its surroundings.

We've got a couple of artists in the family so may share their creations too. They won't necessarily be of Blackpool, but will have been created there so hopefully you won't mind if we share them here.
