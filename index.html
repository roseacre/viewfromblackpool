Our Town

This will be a space for us to share pictures and some historical snippets about the Fylde Coast.

We'll share details of our attempts to adopt a more sustainable lifestyle, through participation in Ikea's Live Lagom programme and based on our own ideas and research.

We'll also save some space for our pets to air their views on the World and each other. Really. They're very opinionated!

Mostly, it'll be a place for a software developer to practice new techniques and try out new software. The content will grow as the training courses are attempted. So please be patient.
